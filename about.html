What is the Bullshido Way?

The Bullshido Method™©® is a deadly and effective fighting discipline created by Coach Larry B and Sensei Steven Seagal during the filming of "End of A Gun". Coach Larry B met Seagal by a twist of fate. Larry had recently been evicted and was forced to sleep on the mats of his dojo, but soon after Larry was contacted by the "End of A Gun" film crew for permission to use his dojo in the straight to dvd and 5 dollar bargain bin blockbuster hit "End of A Gun". By this fateful meeting of luck Larry and Sensei Seagal began devising a new martial arts based on the most effective techniques from various other forms of fighting. Enter The Bullshido Method, the brain child of the copious efforts of Larry and Seagal. After much deliberation, as highly coveted as this deadly art is, Coach Larry B and Sensei Seagal came to a consensus that they would share their ancient wisdom to the public for the greater good of the land. Consider yourself lucky to be alive in such a time to witness such perfection!
